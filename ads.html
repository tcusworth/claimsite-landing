Questions

Common questions

Do I need a Google Business Profile already?

Yes — we build your site from your existing listing data. If you're not on Google Business yet, set that up first (it's free) and come back. It takes about 10 minutes.

What does "free hosting forever" actually mean?

Your site lives on our infrastructure at no ongoing cost to you. You pay once — we host it for as long as ClaimSite exists. No annual renewals, no surprise bills.

What if I already have a domain?

Standard and Pro plans support custom domains. If you already own one (e.g. from GoDaddy or Namecheap), we'll walk you through pointing it to your ClaimSite. If you don't have one yet, we'll let you know where to buy it — they're typically $12–15/year.

How do you build the site without me filling out forms?

We pull your business name, address, category, phone, hours, and reviews directly from your public Google listing. For Pro plans we also do a short intake call to get your voice and story right.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. If you start on Essentials and want a custom domain later, you can upgrade by paying the difference. Just email us after purchase.

Who builds this — is it AI-generated?

Trevor Cusworth, a software builder based in Boulder, CO. AI tools are part of the workflow, but every site is reviewed and finished by a human before it goes live. The Pro plan includes a custom copy pass specifically to remove the AI-generated feel.
